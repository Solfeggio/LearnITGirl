Keeping your body FIT!

Our body is our santuary and to keep on going well we need to take care of it. Eating the right food, exercising, taking care of ourselves in every way are some of the ways to love our body.
Some tips:

  1. Eat in portion pf 1/2 of vegetables and grains, 1/4 meat and 1/4 liquids for balance diet
  2. Exercise about 1 hour each day
  3. Rest well and sleep at least 6 hours per day

 

Power up your MIND

Getting your mind in tip top shape by eating the right brain food and practising mindfulness for optimum conditioning are 2 mind powerful ways.

  • Ensure enough vitamin B and magnesium for better brain power
  • Practising mindfulness and mediation daily for better focus and calmness
